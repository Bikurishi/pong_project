# Neon Pong: Soul Quest

![Neon Pong](https://img.shields.io/badge/Status-Live-brightgreen)

A high-octane, boss-rush take on the classic game of Pong! Defeat increasingly difficult bosses, steal their powers, and master your abilities in this neon-drenched arcade experience featuring a procedural synthesizer soundtrack.

## 🎮 Play the Game
**[Play Neon Pong: Soul Quest Here!](https://Bikurishi.github.io/pong_project/)**

## 📖 How to Play
- **Movement:** Move your paddle to deflect the ball past the boss.
- **Goal:** Score points to defeat the boss and unlock their unique ability.
- **Abilities:** As you progress, you will unlock new powers to help you survive the harder levels!

### Controls & Abilities
- **Move:** Mouse / Touch drag
- **E:** Dash (Unlocks after Level 1)
- **Q:** Blink (Unlocks after Level 2)
- **Spacebar:** Shield (Unlocks after Level 3)
- **Shift:** Chrono Rift (Unlocks after Level 4)
- **P:** Pause Game

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **HTML5 Canvas:** For rendering the high-speed neon graphics.
- **Vanilla JavaScript:** Powers the entire game engine, boss AI, and physics.
- **Web Audio API:** Generates dynamic, procedural synthesized music that changes based on the boss you're fighting.

## 🚀 Local Development
1. Clone the repository: `git clone https://github.com/Bikurishi/pong_project.git`
2. Open `index.html` in your favorite web browser. No local server required!
